# Travel Reimbursement Policy

## 1. Hotel Expenses
- Maximum reimbursement: ₹7000 per night.
- Luxury hotels are not allowed unless pre-approved.
- Hotel receipt is mandatory.

---

## 2. Flight Expenses
- Economy class airfare is fully reimbursable.
- Business class airfare requires Director approval. If not pre-approved, the airfare is not reimbursable.
- Flight ticket/invoice is mandatory.
- Flight reimbursement is based on the actual fare paid.

---

## 3. Meal Expenses
- Maximum reimbursement: ₹1000 per day.
- Alcohol expenses are not reimbursable.
- Meal receipt is mandatory.

---

## 4. Taxi Expenses
- Maximum reimbursement: ₹5000.
- Taxi receipts are mandatory.

---

## 5. Personal Expenses
The following are NOT reimbursable:

- Shopping
- Entertainment
- Personal gifts
- Alcohol

---

## 6. Approval Rules

Claims up to ₹10,000
→ Manager Approval

Claims between ₹10,001 and ₹50,000
→ Senior Manager Approval

Above ₹50,000
→ Director Approval

---

## 7. Missing Documents

If mandatory receipts are missing, receipt attachments are missing, or no receipts are uploaded for reimbursable expenses:

Decision:
Manual Review

---

## 8. Duplicate Receipts

Duplicate receipts must be rejected.

---

If policy information is incomplete or conflicting:
